Welcome

Ray Villalobos is a full-time author and teacher at Lynda.com with over 30 courses focusing on the front-end of the web including JavaScript, Responsive CSS, Sass, Compass, CoffeeScript and Workflows plus libraries and frameworks like Angular, Advanced Bootstrap, Gulp, Reveal, Grunt and others.

He loves to teach and help other by sharing what he knows and exploring new technologies on the web. He is also the author of the book, Exploring Multimedia for Designers.

He has more than 20 years experience in developing and programming interactive projects. As the Director of Multimedia for Entravision Communications, he designed and developed a network of radio station and TV web sites. As a Senior Producer for Tribune Interactive, he was responsible for designing orlandosentinel.com and for creating immersive interactive projects and Flash games for the Tribune network of websites. You can follow Ray @planetoftheweb on Twitter or check out his blog iViewSource.
